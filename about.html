About Us

Empowering Sierra Leone's Football Talent

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To develop and promote Sierra Leone's football talent by providing professional representation, career guidance, and international opportunities that showcase the best of our nation's sporting potential.

Our Vision

To be the leading football agency in West Africa, recognized for developing world-class players and contributing to the growth of football in Sierra Leone and the broader African continent.

Our Story

2020

Foundation

Founded Football Agency Sierra Leone as the first dedicated football agency in the country. Signed our first player and began building relationships with local clubs.

2021

Expansion

Expanded our roster to 10 players and facilitated our first international transfer, establishing connections with European clubs.

2022

Partnerships

Formed strategic partnerships with European clubs and launched our youth development program to nurture the next generation of talent.

2023

Growth

Represented 25+ professional players and established a comprehensive international network spanning multiple continents.

2024

Recognition

Certified by the Sierra Leone Football Association and recognized as the leading football agency in the country.
