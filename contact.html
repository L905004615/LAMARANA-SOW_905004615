Frequently Asked Questions

A: Contact us through our form or call our office. We'll schedule an evaluation session to assess your potential and discuss representation opportunities.

A: We provide player representation, contract negotiations, transfer facilitation, career development guidance, and international exposure opportunities.
Q: Do you work with international clubs?
A: Yes, we have partnerships with clubs in Europe, Asia, and other African countries, facilitating international transfers for our players.
